Food donation is a practical step toward reducing food waste and achieving optimal resource utilization. It helps redistribute surplus edible food instead of discarding it as waste.
It supports vulnerable communities and strengthens individuals' resilience to food insecurity.
It also contributes to reducing the environmental impact of producing and spoiling unused food.
Food donation is thus an effective tool in sustainable development and responsible consumption efforts.
