First: What is food waste?

Food waste is the discarding of food fit for human consumption, whether during production, storage, transportation, retail outlets, or even within homes and restaurants.

Second: The Environmental Impact of Food Waste

  • Increased carbon emissions: Decomposing food produces methane gas, which contributes to climate change.
  • Depletion of natural resources: such as water, energy, and land used for food production.
  • Soil and water pollution: due to food decomposition in landfills.

Third: The Socio-Economic Impact

The discrepancy between surplus food in some countries and hunger in others and huge financial losses globally.

Conclusion:

Food waste harms the environment and the economy and increases inequality. With awareness and conscious action, we can reduce this phenomenon and build a better future.
